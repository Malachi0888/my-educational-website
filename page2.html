The History of Baseball

Origins

Baseball originated in the 19th century in the United States. Early forms of the game were influenced by British bat-and-ball games like rounders.

Major League Baseball

The first professional baseball league was established in 1871. Today, Major League Baseball (MLB) includes 30 teams and has fans worldwide.

Famous Players
